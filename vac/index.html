Covid-19 vaccinations are safe and effective. More information is available here on how Covid-19 vaccines and treatments are approved in Canada. For a list of completed and ongoing clinical trials for current and future Covid-19 treatments, see the Government of Canada's list of authorized clinical trials.

Currently there are five vaccines and four treatments approved for use in Canada. They currently include:

  • Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine (tozinameran) approved for use on December 9, 2020.
  • COVID-19 Vaccine Moderna (mRNA-1273 SARS-CoV-2) approved for use on December 26, 2020.
  • COVISHIELD (ChAdOx1-S [recombinant]) approved for use on February 26, 2021.
  • AstraZeneca COVID-19 Vaccine (ChAdOx1-S [recombinant]) approved for use on February 26, 2021.
  • Janssen COVID-19 Vaccine (Ad26.COV2.S [recombinant]) approved for use on March 5, 2021.
